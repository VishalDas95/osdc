About Mozilla Punjab

Introduction

Mozilla Punjab is a community for technophiles who like technology and for anyone who wants to support Open source. We aim to promote Collective intelligence and Open Source projects among students and other contributors in tech. We are a group of enthusiastic people ranging from students to professional experts willing to teach and make people thrive in Open source.

Frequenty asked Questions(FAQs)

Q. How Mozilla Punjab started?

So we weren’t Mozilla Punjab from the beginning, when we were null, we called ourselves Open Source Developers. Eventually we started exploring the world of Open Source and Mozilla, and that’s how we came around Mozilla Communities. We reached out to Mozilla India and talked to them about starting a Campus Club since we were just students learning about Open Source but, they offered us to start a regional chapter as Mozilla Punjab, so we could bring all the Universities under one umbrella, and make a bigger community! So hey if you made this far, come in and join us and be a part of this amazing Open Community of Punjab!

Q. What is the aim of Mozilla?

Our mission is to ensure the Internet is a global public resource, open and accessible to all. An Internet that truly puts people first, where individuals can shape their own experience and are empowered, safe and independent.
At Mozilla, we’re a global community of technologists, thinkers and builders working together to keep the Internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web. We believe this act of human collaboration across an open platform is essential to individual growth and our collective future.

Q. Who should I contact to be a part of Mozilla Punjab?

No one. If you're polite and helpful or you want to be helped, you're free to ask any questions in the mailing list, google group, telegram group or social accounts.

Q. Who all can contribute to Mozilla Pujab?

In this era of competition everyone is busy in getting the first place, competition with others and letting them down for putting yourself at first place.
This community is for everyone except those.
Everyone can contribute to Mozilla Punjab. We cordially invite contributions and participants from every community and sect regardless of gender, color or any other factor.
Coming together is a beginning. Keeping together is progress. Working together is success.

Q. What I will get by joining any community?

Community gives you Exposure, Expert Teachings, Hackathons, Week of Coding, Conferences, Talks, Hands-on, Projects to contribute and everything that you need to thrive in the era of technology.

Q. What are the domains in focus of the community?

Community gives you a fantastic opportunity to explore and grow in the domain of Open Source Technologies, Data Science, ML, Competitive Programming, Web Development, Security, other domains that Computer Science offers and the list will keep on increasing with time.
